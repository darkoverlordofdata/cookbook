Ramen


Amount Ingredient


meat for stock

6 cups water

50 grams ginger

3 cloves garlic

1 leek


4 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp sake (or ½ mirin ½ white wine)

½ tsp salt

1 tsp sesame oil


Cooking Instructions:


Combine first set of ingredients bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 2 hours. If using pre-made stock, combine with water in a ratio of 2:1 stock to water and simmer for 1 hour.

Skim junk from stock.

Strain stock thru cheese cloth.

To the strained stock, add the second set of ingredients and return to simmer.


To serve add to a bowl

  • noodles

  • boiled eggs

  • bean sprouts

  • bbq pork

  • green onlions

  • etc

and pour soup over everything in the bowl.
