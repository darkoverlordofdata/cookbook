Orange Carnitas (Pork)



Amount Ingredient


1 LB Bone-in Pork Shoulder or Butt, fat trimmed


2 tsp Orange Zest

2 cups Orange Juice [fresh squeezed and bottled) – divided

1 – .5 lb Onion, chopped

1.5 tsp New Mexican Chili

1 Pascilla Pepper


1 15 oz Can Black Beans, rinsed and drained


8 10 inch Flour Tortillas (warmed)

1/3 Cup Green Onions, thinly sliced (white and top)

1/3 Cup Agria

1/3 Cup Tomato Sauce

Salt


Cooking Instructions:


Cut pork into ¼ to ½ inch cubes


Cook on High Heat, stirring often, until pork is browned and medium rare, about 5 – 6 mins


Add

Orange zest,

1 Cup of Orange Juice (save ½ Cup fressh squeezed for finishing)

New Mexican Chili

Pascilla Pepper


Cook over Medium-High Heat until liquid evaporated and meat is lightly browned,

about 20 minutes


Add

½ Cup Orange Juice, scrape browned bits free, stir until Juice evaporated, about 5 minutes


Add

Black Beans


Stir just until Beans are warm, about 2 minutes


Add

Remaining ½ Cup Fresh Squeezed Orange Juice


Serve Pork in warm Tortillas, with Green Onions, Agria, Salsa and Salt to taste.
