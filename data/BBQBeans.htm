BarBQ Beans (Jan’s)



Amount Ingredient


1 8 oz Can B & M Baked Beans

2 Tbs Molasses

3 Tbs Brown Sugar

2 tsp Dijon Mustard

1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce

½ Tbs Tiger Sauce


Optional:

Bacon Bits

Dried Minced Onion



Cooking Instructions:


Add all ingredients to a sauce pan. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for minimum of 15 minutes. Can be kept on low longer.
