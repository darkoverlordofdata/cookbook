**BBQ Seasoning**



[Amount]() [Ingredient]()


1 ½ tsp Salt

¾ tsp Pepper


1 Tbs Garlic Granules

2 Tbs Onion Powder

1 Tbs Smoked Paprika

1 Tbs Brown Sugar

Pinch Cumin

Pinch Citric Acid



[Cooking Instructions:]()


Combine ingredients in a bowl and mix well.


Use on BBQ Potato Chip Chicken
