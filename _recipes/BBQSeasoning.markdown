# BBQ Seasoning  
  
for Potato Chip Chicken  
  
|Amount|Ingredient|  
|----|----|  
1 ½ tsp | Salt  
¾ tsp | Pepper  
1 Tbsp | Garlic Granules  
2 Tbsp | Onion Powder  
1 Tbsp | Smoked Paprika  
1 Tbsp | Brown Sugar  
Pinch | Cumin  
Pinch | Citric Acid  
  
## Cooking Instructions  
Combine ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
