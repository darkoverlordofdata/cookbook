**BBQ Seasoning**

*AmountIngredient*

1 ½ tsp Salt

¾ tspPepper

1 Tbs Garlic Granules

2 TbsOnion Powder

1 TbsSmoked Paprika

1 TbsBrown Sugar

PinchCumin

PinchCitric Acid

Cooking Instructions:

Combine ingredients in a bowl and mix well.

Use on BBQ Potato Chip Chicken
