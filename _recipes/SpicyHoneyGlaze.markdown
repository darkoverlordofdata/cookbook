**Spicy Honey Glaze**

*AmountIngredient*

½ CupCider Vinegar

½ CupHoney

2 TbsPacked Brown Sugar

2 TbsVegetable Oil

2 -- 3 tspDry Mustard (Coleman's)

1/8 tspGround ginger

DashCayenne Pepper

Cooking Instructions:

In saucepan, combine ingredients, mixing well.

Bring to a boil.

Reduce heat and simmer about 10 minutes, until slightly thickened.

Use on ham.
