# Asian Salad Dressing  
  
|Amount|Ingredient|  
||----|----||  
½ Cup | mayonnaise  
1 ½ tsp | soy sauce  
¼ tsp | sesame oil  
1 ½ Tbsp | unseasoned rice wine vinegar  
2 Tbsp | mirin  
  
## Cooking Instructions  
Whisk together, store in a squeeze bottle
