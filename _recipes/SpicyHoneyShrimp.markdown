**Spicy Honey Shrimp **

*AmountIngredient*

½ CupHoney

¼ CupSoy Sauce

1 tspSesame Seeds

2 ClovesGarlic

¼ tspChili Paste

1 LbShrimp (Fresh or Frozen)

Cooking Instructions:

Combine sauce ingredients and pour over Shrimp

Broil 6-7 Minutes or until shrimp cooked
