# Lamb Marinade Sicillian  
  
|Amount|Ingredient|  
|----|----|  
¼ Cup | Olive Oil  
½ Cup | Red Wine Vinegar  
|| Basil  
|| Lemon Pepper  
|| Onion Paste  
|| Garlic - minced  
  
## Cooking Instructions  
Combine ingredients.  
  
Baste on lamb chops or roast. Use remaining as sauce at table.
