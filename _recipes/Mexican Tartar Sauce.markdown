# Mexican Tartar Sauce  
  
|Amount|Ingredient|  
|----|----|  
½ Cup | Sour Cream  
¼ Cup | Mayonaise  
1 Clove | Garlic  
½ | Lime Juice  
1 Tbsp | Hot Sauce (Hot Ortega Taco Sauce)  
  
## Cooking Instructions  
Blend ingredients, stirring until well combined. Pour into squeeze bottle.
