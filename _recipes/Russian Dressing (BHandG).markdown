# Russian Dressing (BH&G)  
  
|Amount|Ingredient|  
|----|----|  
⅔ Cup | Salad Oil  
½ Cup | Ketchup  
¼ Cup | Sugar  
3 Tbsp | Lemon Juice  
2 Tbsp | Lee & Perrins  
2 Tbsp | Vinegar  
2 Tbsp | Water  
1 Tbsp | Onion, grated  
½ tsp | Salt  
½ tsp | Paprika  
  
## Cooking Instructions  
  
Combine ingredients in a jar with a screw-top lid.  
Shake well to mix.  
Refrigerate.  
  
Makes 1 ¾ Cups.
