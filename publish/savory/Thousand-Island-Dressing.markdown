# Thousand Island Dressing

|Amount|Ingredient|
|----|----|
½ Cup | Mayonnaise
2 Tbsp | Ketchup
1 Tbsp | White Vinegar
2 tsp | Sugar
2 tsp | Sweet Pickle Relish
1 tsp | Minced Onion (dry)
⅛ tsp | Salt
Dash | Black Pepper

## Cooking Instructions

Combine ingredients.
Let sit over night for best flavor
