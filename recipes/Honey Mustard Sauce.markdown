# Honey Mustard Sauce  
  
|Amount|Ingredient|  
|----|----|  
¼ Cup | Dijon Mustard  
⅜ Cup | Honey  
½ tsp | Garlic Powder  
½ tsp | Onion Powder  
|| Salt  
|| Black Pepper  
  
## Cooking Instructions  
Combine ingredients in bowl. Wisk together.  
  
Baste on chicken or chops before cooking.  
  
Can set aside some sauce to serve with dinner.
