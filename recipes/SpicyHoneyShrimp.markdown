# Spicy Honey Shrimp  
  
|Amount|Ingredient|  
|----|----|  
½ Cup | Honey  
¼ Cup | Soy Sauce  
1 tsp | Sesame Seeds  
2 Cloves | Garlic  
¼ tsp | Chili Paste  
1 lb | Shrimp (Fresh or Froz | en)  
  
## Cooking Instructions  
Combine sauce ingredients and pour over Shrimp  
  
Broil 6-7 Minutes or until shrimp cooked
