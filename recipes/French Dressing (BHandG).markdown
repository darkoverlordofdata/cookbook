# French Dressing (BH&G)  
  
|Amount|Ingredient|  
|----|----|  
½ Cup | Salad Oil  
2 Tbsp | Vinegar  
2 Tbsp | Lemon Juice  
1 tsp | Sugar  
¾ tsp | Dry Mustard  
½ tsp | Salt  
⅛ tsp | Paprika  
Dash | Ground Red Pepper   
  
## Cooking Instructions  
  
In a screw-lid jar, combine all ingredients and shake well to mix.  
Store in refrigerator.  
Makes ¾ Cup.
