**Mexican Tartar Sauce**

**Description:**

For fish and shrimp tacos

**Ingredients List:**

**Amount Of**

1/2 Cup Sour Cream

1/4 Cup Mayonaise

1 Clove Garlic

1/2 Lime Juice

1 Tbs Hot Sauce (Hot Ortega Taco Sauce)

**Instructions:**

Combine ingredients, stirring until well combined. Pour into squeeze
bottle.
