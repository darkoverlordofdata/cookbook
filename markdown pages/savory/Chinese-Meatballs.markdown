# Chinese Meatballs

Oven 400

|Amount|Ingredient|
|----|----|
½ lb. | Ground Beef
½ lb. | Ground Pork
⅛ tsp | Ginger Powder
¼ tsp | Garlic Powder
½ tsp | Onion Powder
½ - ¾ Cups | Bread Crumbs
½ Cup | Oyster Sauce
1 | Egg

## Cooking Instructions

Add ingredients in order listed in a large bowl.
Mix well.
Make small balls (golf ball size), and roll in additional bread crumbs.

Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
