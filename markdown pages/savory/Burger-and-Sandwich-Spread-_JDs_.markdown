# Burger & Sandwich Spread (JD's)

|Amount|Ingredient|
|----|----|
3 Tbsp | Mayonnaise
1 ½  - 2 tsp | Dijon Mustard
1 ½ tsp | Pickle Juice, including bits
|| Salt
|| Black Pepper, freshly ground
|| Garlc Powder

## Cooking Instructions

Combine Ingredients, mixing well.

Store in a screw-lid jar in refrigerator.
